About

Hello! I am an EECS student at UC Berkeley with a strong interest in the device-level foundations of modern electronics. Through coursework such as EE130, I have become increasingly interested in semiconductor physics and the fields that build upon it, including integrated circuit devices and fabrication technologies. I enjoy exploring how physical principles translate into real-world devices and systems, and I am eager to continue learning through advanced coursework, research, and hands-on projects. I am currently seeking opportunities to further develop my understanding of semiconductor-driven technologies and their applications. Additionally, I have always wanted to work in the automotive field for electrical engineer.
